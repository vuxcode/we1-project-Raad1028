Strategy >>

In most strategy games you take control of a country or an army. In some strategy games your goal is to defeat all the opponents in battles, while others are more open ended and you set your own goals.

RPG >>

In an RPG you take control of characters with their own classes and stats. Throughout the game your character gets stronger and you get to allocate stat points to the area you want the character to excel at.

Roguelike

Roguelike >>

A roguelike is a game with nearly infinite replayability. You find unique items in each run but once you die you have to restart from the beginning. Even if you lose you unlock things that can improve your future runs
